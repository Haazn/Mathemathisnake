What is Median in statistics?
Median is the middle score in statistics.

Example:
Sir Jeff recorded his income for 5 days,
his income was 600, 700, 500, 1000,
and 700. What is the median score
of his income for the 5 days?

Solution:
The recorded income was
600, 600, 500, 1000, and 700.
All you have to do is rearrange the
income from lowest to highest.

Therefore, we will arrange them,
we have 500, 600, 600, 700, and 1000.
that means 600 was the Median score.
