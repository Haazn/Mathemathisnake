Greetings, my fellow Adventurer. Ready to learn about triangles?


Triangles are 3-sided polygons with three
angles. The sum of interior angles in any
triangle is always 180°.Triangles can be
classified by sides or angles.

Example:
A triangle has angles measuring 50° and 60°.
What is the measure of the third angle?

Solution:
We know the sum of angles in a triangle is
180°. We already had the first and second
angle which are 50° and 60°

To find the third
angle, we subtract the sum of known
angles from 180°. 180°- 50° + 60° = 110°
180° - 110° = 70°. The third angle is 70°
